About B20 South Africa

The Business 20 (B20) serves as the official G20 dialogue forum with the global business community. Established in 2010, the B20 is one of the most influential G20 Engagement Groups, bringing together business leaders from G20 member countries and beyond. Each year, the B20 provides a platform for companies and business organisations to articulate their perspectives on pressing global economic and trade issues, ensuring that the voice of the business community is heard at the highest levels of international economic governance.

Theme

“Inclusive Growth and Prosperity through Global Cooperation” is the cornerstone of B20 South Africa’s mission. This theme underscores the importance of collaborative efforts in addressing global economic challenges and ensuring that growth benefits all segments of society

Host Organisation

Business Unity South Africa (BUSA) is at the helm of B20 South Africa, leading the charge in mobilising the global business community towards impactful and actionable policy recommendations for the G20. BUSA’s commitment to inclusive growth and prosperity is reflected in its efforts to engage diverse business stakeholders in meaningful dialogue and consensus-building

Mxolisi Mgojo

B20 Co-chair

In a world brimming with challenges, there is also immense opportunity. South Africa's presidency of the G20 is a moment to inspire hope and unity, showcasing the power of collaboration to drive meaningful and inclusive growth.

We are proud to take the helm of the B20, embracing this role as a call to action to deliver measurable outcomes and build on the momentum of the recent Global South presidencies. Our vision for the B20 is to be a catalyst for collaboration and innovation, first between the global business community and the world’s policymakers, and then between the Global South and the North. We’ll continue to fight for the rights of women and climate resilience and help guide the policies that will have an impact on the lives of nearly five billion people.

To every business leader across Africa and the globe, this is an open invitation to join us to deliver sustainable and transformative change.

Nonkululeko Nyembezi

B20 Co-chair

South Africa’s G20 presidency marks a bold and transformative moment for Africa on the global stage, showcasing the continent’s readiness to lead and collaborate on the world’s most pressing challenges. At the heart of this presidency lies B20 South Africa, a dynamic platform where the private sector drives solutions to create inclusive growth and prosperity through global collaboration.

Our theme reflects a commitment to fostering innovation, unlocking investment, and building resilient economies that benefit all. This is a unique opportunity to shape the global agenda, amplify Africa’s voice in business leadership, and create partnerships that deliver real impact.

We invite the global business community to join us in this exciting journey to turn vision into action and ambition into achievement. Together, we can create a future of shared success, inclusion, and opportunity for all.
